About Online Manipal

Online Manipal University is a top-notch University for online degree courses. Online Manipal is Approved by the University Grants Commission (UGC) and All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), showing its dedication to maintaining high educational standards. The University offers a wide variety of accredited degree courses across different specializations, making education accessible to all students. With its flexible online degree programs, It allows students to engage with course materials, lectures, & interactive sessions and students can learn from anywhere.
